1. GENERAL SYSTEM OVERVIEW
--------------------------
TransitLK is a comprehensive bus booking and fleet management platform designed to streamline public transport in Sri Lanka. It connects passengers with bus operators, offering real-time seat booking, secure payments, and live bus tracking.

System Roles:
- Passenger: Searches and books bus tickets.
- Operator: Manages bus fleet, routes, schedules, and fares.
- Administrator: Oversees the entire platform, users, and generates reports.

---

2. FOR PASSENGERS (PUBLIC USERS)
--------------------------------
As a passenger, you can use TransitLK to plan your journey and secure your seat in advance.

2.1. Getting Started
   - Visit the home page to access the main search interface.
   - You can search for buses without an account, but booking requires signing in.
   - Click "Sign In" or "Register" to create an account for booking history and profile management.

2.2. Booking a Ticket
   Step 1: Search
   - Enter your "From" (Start Location) and "To" (End Location).
   - Select your "Journey Date".
   - Click "Search Buses".

   Step 2: Select Bus
   - Review available buses, departure times, and ticket prices.
   - Click "Select" on your preferred bus.

   Step 3: Choose Seats
   - A seat layout will appear.
   - Click on available seats (white/grey) to select them. Selected seats will change color.
   - Review the total price.

   Step 4: Payment
   - Proceed to checkout.
   - Enter payment details (Credit/Debit Card, etc.).
   - Confirm payment.

   Step 5: Confirmation
   - Upon successful payment, you will receive a digital ticket with a QR code.
   - You can view this ticket in your "My Bookings" section.

2.3. Dashboard Features
   - My Bookings: View upcoming and past trips.
   - Profile: Update your personal details.
   - Tracking: Track your assigned bus in real-time (if available for the route).

3. FOR BUS OPERATORS
--------------------
Operators have access to a dedicated dashboard to manage their fleet and revenue.

3.1. Dashboard Overview
   - Login to the Operator Portal.
   - The main dashboard displays key metrics: Total Revenue, Active Buses, Today's Bookings.

3.2. Fleet Management (Buses)
   - Navigate to the "Buses" section.
   - Add New Bus: Enter Bus Number, Capacity, Model, and Amenities.
   - Edit/Delete: Manage existing bus details.
   - Device Assignment: Link GPS tracking devices to buses for real-time tracking.

3.3. Route & Schedule Management
   - Routes: Define routes with Start/End locations and intermediate stops.
   - Schedules: Assign buses to specific routes and set Departure/Arrival times.
   - Fares: Set ticket prices for different routes and bus types in the "Fare" section.

3.4. Bookings Management
   - View a list of all bookings for your buses.
   - Filter by date, route, or status (Confirmed, Pending).
   - Verify passenger tickets using QR code scanning (if applicable).

3.5. Live Tracking
   - Use the "Tracking" feature to see the real-time location of all your active buses on a map.

---

4. FOR ADMINISTRATORS
---------------------
Administrators have full control over the system configuration and data.

4.1. Admin Dashboard
   - Provides a high-level view of system health.
   - Key User Statistics, Total Bookings, and Platform Revenue.

4.2. User Management
   - Navigate to "Users" to manage passenger and operator accounts.
   - View user details, booking history, and account status.
   - Deactivate or ban users violating terms.

4.3. Analytics & Reporting
   - Analytics: Visual charts showing booking trends, revenue growth, and popular routes.
   - Reports: Generate detailed reports for specific periods (Daily, Monthly) for auditing and analysis.

4.4. System Settings
   - Manage global configurations.
